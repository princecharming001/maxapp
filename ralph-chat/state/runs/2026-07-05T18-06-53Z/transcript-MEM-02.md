## turn 6 ('what causes dandruff')
**user:** what causes dandruff
**assistant:** dandruff is usually caused by malassezia (a yeast on your scalp) overgrowth triggered by oil, dry scalp, or inflammation. stress and cold weather make it worse.

the standard fix is antifungal shampoo. your best options are:

**zinc pyrithione shampoo** — use 2-3x per week for 2 weeks, then drop to 1x per week maintenance.

**ketoconazole 2%** — if the zinc pyrithione isn't working after 2 weeks.

avoid any shampoos labeled as exfoliating or "2-in-1" formulas, as these often contain ingredients that can irritate sensitive scalps.

also:
1. **dry your scalp fully** after showering (malassezia thrives in damp conditions)
2. **keep stress levels down**
3. **avoid heavy oils on** the scalp until it clears
4. **if it doesn't improve** in 4 weeks, see a dermatologist—could be seborrheic dermatitis instead

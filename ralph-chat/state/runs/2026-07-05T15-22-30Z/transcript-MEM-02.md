treatment depends on the cause. if it's malassezia, an antifungal shampoo with zinc pyrithione or ketoconazole works fast. if it's dryness, you need a gentler shampoo plus scalp oil. if it's irritation from harsh sulfates or fragrance, switch to a simple, fragrance-free formula.

for your needs, go for zinc pyrithione or ketoconazole-based shampoos—these are antifungal options that won't trigger sensitivity issues. both work well without exfoliating ingredients that might irritate your scalp.

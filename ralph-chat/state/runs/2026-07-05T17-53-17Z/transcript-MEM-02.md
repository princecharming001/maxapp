## turn 6 ('what causes dandruff')
**user:** what causes dandruff
**assistant:** dandruff usually comes from one of three things: dry scalp, fungal overgrowth (malassezia), or sensitivity to hair products.

better moves:

1. **switch shampoo** to something gentle and sulfate-free. fungal dandruff responds well to zinc pyrithione or ketoconazole shampoos. use 2–3×/week.

2. **scalp health** — massage your scalp during wash to improve blood flow and reduce buildup. don't scratch or pick.

3. **moisturize the scalp** with a lightweight oil (jojoba or argan) post-shower on damp hair, focusing on the roots. counterintuitive but works.

4. **diet** — omega-3s and b vitamins help. make sure you're eating enough fat and getting micronutrients.

if it persists after 2–3 weeks, it might be seborrheic dermatitis (fungal), which needs a dermatologist's call. but start with a ketoconazole shampoo and scalp massage first.

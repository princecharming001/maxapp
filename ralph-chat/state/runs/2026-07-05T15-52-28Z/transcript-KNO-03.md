**assistant:** mewing is tongue posture training. you press your tongue flat against the roof of your mouth (back third), seal your lips, and keep your teeth in light contact. the idea is that sustained tongue-up posture changes how your jaw and midface sit over time. does it work for adults? marginally. mewing alone won't reshape bone if your sutures are closed (which they are at 33). what it does do is train postural awareness and reduce forward head posture, which makes your jawline read sharper because your chin isn't tilted down. think of it as a baseline habit, not a standalone fix. the real jaw change comes from masseter training (chewing hard gum, jawzrsize) and dropping body fat below 15%. those move the needle visibly in 8-12 weeks. mewing is the foundation you stack on top of that, not the main lever. the protocol is 3 daily resets: 60s active hold at wake, 30s reset at midday, and a night check 30 min before bed. passive posture all day in between.

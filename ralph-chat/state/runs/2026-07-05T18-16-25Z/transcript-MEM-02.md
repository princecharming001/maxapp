## turn 6 ('what causes dandruff')
**user:** what causes dandruff
**assistant:** dandruff is usually caused by one of three things: dry scalp (especially in winter or with harsh shampoos), malassezia yeast overgrowth (thrives in oily scalp conditions), or sensitivity to sulfates/silicones in your products. since you have oily skin, you're more prone to the yeast route. treatment depends on the cause—if it's dry, use a hydrating shampoo and skip hot water. if it's yeast-driven, look for zinc pyrithione or ketoconazole shampoos (anti-fungal). if it's product buildup, switch to a gentler sulfate-free formula. scalp health also ties to diet—high sugar and processed foods feed yeast, so cutting those helps skin and scalp both.

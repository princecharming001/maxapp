treatment: use a zinc pyrithione or ketoconazole shampoo 2–3x per week (kills the yeast), then switch to a gentle sulfate-free shampoo on other days. keep your scalp moisturized with a lightweight oil or scalp serum between washes. avoid hot water, which dries you out. if it's really stubborn, a dermatologist can prescribe a stronger antifungal.

stick with zinc pyrithione or ketoconazole-based products—these are your best options and won't irritate your scalp.

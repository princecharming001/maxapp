**assistant:** mewing is tongue posture training. you put your tongue on the roof of your mouth (back third), seal your lips, and keep your teeth in light contact. the idea is that sustained tongue-up posture + sealed lips + slight chin tuck trains your jaw and midface over time. does it work for adults. not really. your bone is mostly locked by 25. what mewing can do is reinforce posture habits that keep your jaw from sagging forward, which helps your jawline read sharper. but the actual structural change is marginal. the real jaw-building move is masseter training (thick chewing gum, jawzrsize, etc). that produces visible change in 8-12 weeks because you're actually hypertrophying muscle, not waiting for bone remodeling. if you want to move the needle on your jawline, start there instead. if you want to add mewing anyway, the protocol is 3 daily resets: morning at wake (60s active hold, then passive all day), midday reset (30s conscious check), and night check 30min before bed.

## turn 6 ('what causes dandruff')
**user:** what causes dandruff
**assistant:** dandruff is usually caused by one of three things: malassezia (a yeast that thrives on scalp oil), dry scalp from harsh shampoos or low humidity, or seborrheic dermatitis (an inflammatory condition). since you have oily skin, malassezia is your most likely culprit — it feeds on sebum.

treatment depends on the root cause. if it's yeast-related, a zinc pyrithione or ketoconazole shampoo kills malassezia. if it's dry scalp, you need a gentler cleanser and more moisture. if it's seborrheic dermatitis, you'd want an anti-inflammatory like sulfur, ketoconazole, or piroctone olamine, which target the inflammation without irritating your skin.

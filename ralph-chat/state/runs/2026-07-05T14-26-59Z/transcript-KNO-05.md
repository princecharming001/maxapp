4. **sleep 7–9 hours** — recovery is when muscle builds and hormones regulate. you're already set with 23:00–07:00, keep it.

5. **stay consistent for 12+ weeks** — you won't see dramatic change in 2 weeks. the body responds to time under tension and sustained deficit, not intensity sprints.

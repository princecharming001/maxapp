## turn 6 ('what causes dandruff')
**user:** what causes dandruff
**assistant:** dandruff usually comes from one of three things: a yeast called malassezia overgrowth (most common), dry scalp, or sensitivity to hair products. malassezia feeds on scalp oil, so oily scalp guys like you are more prone to it.

treatment depends on the cause. if it's yeast-driven, you need an antifungal shampoo with zinc pyrithione or ketoconazole. if it's dry scalp, you need moisture and less frequent washing. if it's product sensitivity, strip back to basics—gentle shampoo, rinse well, don't use heavy conditioners on your scalp.

since you're starting hairmax soon and have oily skin, i'd lean toward the malassezia angle. grab a zinc pyrithione shampoo, use it 2–3x a week, and keep scalp washes quick—don't let conditioner sit on your scalp. if it doesn't clear in 2 weeks, switch to ketoconazole.
